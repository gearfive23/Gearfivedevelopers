Our Projects

Disaster Management using Drone

Using drone technology for disaster management, we help locate victims and deliver supplies in emergencies.

With two drones, we map locations and gather crucial data for rescue efforts. Drone 1 detects people and Drone 2 delivers supplies based on the information gathered.

Smart Emergency Messenger

Our messenger automatically detects emergencies and sends real-time alerts, enhancing safety through quick response systems.

It includes features like location tracking for remote areas and accident detection to provide immediate assistance.

Anti-Drone Technology

This project involves advanced tracking and neutralization techniques for drones, aimed at protecting critical areas from UAV threats.

We use sensors and machine learning algorithms to detect and disable unauthorized drones efficiently.
